About Me


Hello There, my name is Elio. Thanks for taking a look at my work. I was born in East Los Angeles, but I grew up all over Los Angeles. When I'm not designing a website or illustrating, you can catch me in my garage working on my car, or cruising around LA to find my next inspiration for my illustrations.


I'm currently enrolled in the Graphics Communications program at California State University, Los Angeles. I built this website from my learnings in my HTML class. I'm constantly updating my gallery with new works when I have new content to add.

Thank you for checking out my works!

If you'd like to contact me about my work, send me an Email and I'll reply to you as soon as posible.
